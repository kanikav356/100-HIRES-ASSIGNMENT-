** Original Post Content: 
Types of content you need to build a brand. 

How I've grown to 100k & built a 6-figure business alongside.

1. Visibility Content:

For: New audience to discover you. 
Goal: Get more profile views.

Carousels, tool lists, swipe files, cheatsheets, workflows, industry news.

2. Trust Content:

For: People who know you but need convincing.
Goal: Prove you can solve their problems.

Storytelling, challenges, successes, templates, frameworks, case studies, strategy, systems.

3. Conversion Content:

For: People who trust you and are ready to buy.
Goal: Remove doubt and get them to reach out.

Offer breakdowns, waitlists, testimonials, resources, coaching, lead magnets, referrals, workshops.

By posting all 3 is how you get your audience to:
1. Discover you.
2. Trust you.
3. Buy from you.

** Annotation: Explains a three-stage LinkedIn content framework (Visibility, Trust, Conversion) that maps content types to different stages of the buyer journey. Useful for designing balanced LinkedIn content strategies that grow reach while nurturing trust and generating inbound leads.

** Original Post Content: 
90% of LinkedIn posts are written for the author.

The top 10% are written for the reader.

Here are 5 ways to join the top 10%:

1. Lead with their problem
→ Name their frustration in your hook.
→ "I hit 10K" is about you. "How you can too" is about them.

2. Give away your best stuff
→ Post the frameworks you'd charge for.
→ Generosity builds trust faster than pitches.

3. Answer comments with depth
→ "Thanks!" is forgettable. 
→ A 3 sentence answer with a tip gets remembered.

4. Share your mistakes
→ Your failures help someone else learn.
→ Vulnerability builds connection faster than credentials.

5. Celebrate others
→ Support small accounts! 
→ Find 5+ active creators to support weekly.

When you focus on helping, the growth follows.

** Annotation: Highlights audience-first content creation, emphasizing value, engagement, community interaction, and authentic storytelling over self-promotional posting. Reinforces that sustainable LinkedIn growth comes from consistently solving audience problems.

Your posts aren't converting?

Try one of these 9 copywriting frameworks.

(Save this for your next content day) ♻️

Great copy isn't about being clever.
It's about using what already works.

Here are 9 formulas that convert:

1. 𝗣𝗔𝗦 (Problem, Agitate, Solution)
→ Name the pain your reader feels.
→ Twist the knife. Make it urgent.
→ Present your solution as the relief.

2. 𝗔𝗜𝗗𝗔 (Attention, Interest, Desire, Action)
→ Hook them with a bold opener.
→ Build curiosity with benefits.
→ End with a clear next step.

3. 𝗕𝗔𝗕 (Before, After, Bridge)
→ Show where they are now (the struggle).
→ Paint where they could be (the dream).
→ Your offer is the bridge between.

4. 𝟰𝗣𝘀 (Promise, Picture, Proof, Push)
→ Make a bold promise.
→ Help them visualize the outcome.
→ Back it up with proof. Then ask for action.

5. 𝗙𝗔𝗕 (Features, Advantages, Benefits)
→ State what it does.
→ Explain why that matters.
→ Show how it changes their life.

6. 𝗧𝗵𝗲 𝟰 𝗨𝘀 (Useful, Urgent, Unique, Ultra-specific)
→ Your headline should check all 4 boxes.
→ Generic = ignored.
→ Specific = clicked.

7. 𝗤𝗨𝗘𝗦𝗧 (Qualify, Understand, Educate, Stimulate, Transition)
→ Filter for the right reader.
→ Show you get their problem.
→ Teach, excite, then guide to action.

8. 𝗦𝗼 𝗪𝗵𝗮𝘁?
→ Write a statement.
→ Ask "so what?" until you hit emotion.
→ That's your real message.

9. 𝟭-𝟮-𝟯-𝟰 (1 Problem, 2 Causes, 3 Solutions, 4 Steps)
→ Use this to structure complex ideas.
→ Breaks down overwhelming topics.
→ Readers love clear frameworks.

** Annotation: Provides nine copywriting frameworks (AIDA, PAS, BAB, QUEST, etc.) adapted for LinkedIn content. Useful for structuring educational, storytelling, and conversion-focused posts that increase engagement and drive action.
